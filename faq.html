Frequently Asked Questions

1. How does Wheel Buddy work?

Wheel Buddy allows users to rent and share cars within their community, offering a simple, secure platform for both car owners and renters.

2. How do I register on Wheel Buddy?

You can sign up using your email or social media accounts through the registration page on our website.

3. How do I rent a car?

After signing in, browse the available cars in your area, select your desired car, and book it for your preferred time period.

4. What payment methods are accepted?

We accept credit/debit cards, PayPal, and other online payment methods for bookings.

5. Can I cancel my booking?

Yes, you can cancel your booking up to 24 hours before the scheduled pickup time for a full refund.

6. Is insurance included in the rental?

Yes, all our rentals come with comprehensive insurance coverage for peace of mind.

7. How do I contact customer support?

You can reach our customer support team via email, phone, or our live chat service on the website.

8. Can I share my car with others?

Yes, you can make your car available for sharing within the community, earning money from your vehicle.

9. How do I manage my bookings?

You can manage your bookings through your user dashboard, where you can view, modify, or cancel your reservations.

10. Are there any age restrictions for renting a car?

Yes, renters must be at least 21 years old with a valid driver's license to rent a car through Wheel Buddy.
